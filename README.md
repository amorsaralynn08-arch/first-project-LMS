# OPPORTUNITY BRIDGE
## welcome to opportunity bridge where dreames are realised
OpportunityBridge is a digital platform designed to connect students with scholarships, grants, internships, and educational opportunities from around the world.  

It aims to make opportunity discovery simple, accessible, and organized for students who want to further their education but struggle to find reliable information.

## ***problem in question***
There is no centralized platform where students can easily discover verified scholarships, grants, and internships.

Most opportunities are:

- Spread across many different websites

 - Shared randomly on social media

- Difficult to aquire due to deadlines and eligibility requirements
- fake

As a result, many students miss important opportunities.

## ___Solution___
OpportunityBridge will provide a centralized website where students can easily discover educational opportunities.

The platform will have
 
 - Searchable opportunity database for genuine scholarships, grants, and internships

- Deadline reminders so students don’t miss applications

- Email notifications when new opportunities are available

- Application guidance to help students understand requirements
